Site Name

Legendary Street Foods

This name was selected because it clearly represents the website's focus on street food from around the world, making it easily recognizable and relevant.

legendarystreetfoods.com

Site Purpose

The website aims to highlight international street food through photos, recipes, localization, and cultural background. It will include three main pages featuring different street foods worldwide, their origins, and how to make them at home.

Scenarios

  • What are some popular street foods in different countries?
  • How can I make a specific street food at home?
  • What are some easy street food recipes for beginners?

Color Schema

  • Seal Brown: Used for headings, accents, and primary elements.
  • Xanthous: Used for buttons, link hover effects, and secondary elements.
  • White: Used for background colors.
  • Black: Used for primary text color for readability.

Typography

  • Roboto: Used for headings and titles with larger weight. Used for body text and paragraphs with smaller weight and additional italic.

Pages

Home

The Home page will provide an overview of the website, featuring highlights of various legendary, shared recipes of the week, a call to action to share their favorite recipe, street foods from around the world, and introducing visitors to the content they can explore on the site.

Food Spots

The Food Spots page will showcase different locations known for their legendary street food. It will include photos, descriptions, and the cultural significance of these food spots, helping visitors learn about and appreciate various street foods globally. It will also filter and display the content by Country.

Recipes

The Recipes page will offer detailed instructions on how to make legendary street foods at home. It will include ingredients, step-by-step preparation methods, and tips to replicate the authentic taste of these street foods. I will also show a subsection of the worldlwide shared recipes by site visitors and collaborators.

Share Recipe

The Share Recipe page will invite visitors to share their own street food recipes through a form. This interactive section will allow food enthusiasts to contribute to the website by providing their unique recipes and stories, fostering a community of street food lovers.
